League Overview

BSFB is a true franchise building experience designed to mimic the NBA as closely as fantasy sports will allow.

Scoring System is Head-to-Head Total Points: Teams compete Head to Head to earn a single Win, Loss or Tie based on which team scores more Fantasy Points in a scoring period. All 30 NBA Franchises will be represented. Full 82 Game Schedule for 12 teams with 8 making the playoffs. Realistic year round off-season activity.

Our League began circa 2007-08 in the droms of Arizona State in Tempe. (The same building that future NBA All-Star James Harden was living). This October we will be entering our 8th season as a league. Below is our "League Constitution", where all league rules and detailes will be housed.

Trades

Any trade can be made after the league draft concludes. Our trade deadline will always be the same as the NBA (usually the nearest Thursday following the All Star Break at 12pm Pacific Standard Time.

Weekly Trade Chat: Before the season begins we will vote on a night to hold weekly trade chats via the League Facebook Group Owners are not reqired to particpate in league chat, but it is highly recoomended to the ingretiy of the league. Any manager can also post the Facebook page of players that you are looking to trade and/or players/postions you need.

Trade Review: All current owners are memebers of the "Trade Review Committee". Any trade that happens during the season will be posted on the Facebook Group Page. If anyone feels the trade is biased or unfair in anyway they can veto the trade. If x amount of managers or % of the league (will vote on each season) veto's the trade with 72 hours it will be cancelled.


Waivers

Weekly Amount: Unlimited

If you pick up a player BEFORE the first game starts on that same day, that player will appear on your roster the following day.

Roster Dumping: is when a manager drops a large quanity of players on the final day in order to reach the player limit on the verge of elimination. We will have a vote to possbibly freeze the top players on a roster of an eliminated team to keep the waiver wire from being populated with unbalanced players that were intially drafted by a diffrent team.

Lineup Management

Owners can set their own lineups, but active rosters lock five (5) minutes prior to tip-off of first game of scoring period.(Mon & Fri) Rosters can not be changed for scoring period once rosters lock.


Most leagues implement more strict guidlines when in comes to position eligiblity slots. Our league belives in more of "postionless" basketball aproach with roster spots.


Mangers are only required to fill one of each of the postions of the 10 max players that can be eligble each day. The next 2 slots are for any gaurd/forward and forward/center with enough crossover that should eliminate roster postion issues. The final 3 spots are FLEX postions that any player is eligble for.


Weekly Player Limit

Regular Season: 40 Players Max.

Playoffs/Post Season: 80 Players Max.

Each team can start a max of 40 players each week. Going over that limit before or after the week is over will result in the HIGHEST scoring player(s) being SUBTRACTED from your total score of the week. It is up to the managers to field exactly their best 40 to avoid the penalty and keeping the league fair.


If a player is placed in a roster spot in your lineup but does NOT play any minutes, then that player will not be added to your teams weekly total.


Please keep in mind that ometimes in league chat the player limit rule is also refered to as "The Lamar Reynolds Rule"
